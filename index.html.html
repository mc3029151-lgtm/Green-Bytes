About Us

Green Bytes is a smart farming platform designed to help farmers detect crop diseases early and take quick action. Our AI-powered system analyzes plant images and provides accurate disease detection along with recommended solutions.

In addition, our integrated marketplace allows farmers to sell their crops directly to buyers, ensuring better prices and eliminating middlemen.

Our mission is to empower farmers with technology, improve crop yield, and build a sustainable agricultural ecosystem.
